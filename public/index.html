About ScholarSeek

🎓

Empowering Students for Success

ScholarSeek is a rule-based scholarship matching platform designed to help Filipino students find scholarships that match their qualifications.

Key Features

  • Smart Matching based on GPA, major, location, and more
  • Profile Management - save and manage your profile
  • Progress Tracking from planning to submission
  • Deadline Alerts so you never miss a deadline
